    Seek Help

    • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255) to speak with a trained counselor 24/7 if you or someone you know is in crisis.
    • Crisis Text Line: Text "HOME" to 741741 to connect with a crisis counselor via text message. Available 24/7.
    • National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) Helpline: Call 1-800-950-NAMI (1-800-950-6264) to get support, information, and resources related to mental health conditions.
    • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) National Helpline: Call 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357) for assistance with mental health and substance abuse issues. Available 24/7.
    • TrevorLifeline (LGBTQ+ Support): Call 1-866-488-7386 for crisis intervention and suicide prevention services for LGBTQ+ youth.
    • National Domestic Violence Hotline: Call 1-800-799-SAFE (1-800-799-7233) for support and resources related to domestic violence and abuse.
    • Veterans Crisis Line: Call 1-800-273-8255 and press 1, or text 838255 to connect with confidential support for veterans and service members.
    • The Crisis Call Center: Call 1-775-784-8090 or text "ANSWER" to 839863 for help with crisis intervention and mental health support.
    • Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline: Call 1-800-4-A-CHILD (1-800-422-4453) for assistance with child abuse and neglect situations.
    • Emergency Services: In life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.
